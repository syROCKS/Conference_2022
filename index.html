JC Bose UST

The University has been accredited by National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) with 'A' Grade and a CGPA of 3.08 in the First Cycle of Accreditation. Established in 1969 as an indo-German project, a joint venture of the National Council of YMCA India, the Government of Haryana and the Central Agencies for Development Aid, Bonn, Germany it was awarded the status of state university by the State Government in December 2009.

paper submission

Prospective authors are invited to submit papers of 6 pages(A4 size) including tables, figues and references, although if you have a paper with more number of pages that you would like to be considered you can submit that as well, All submissions must be made in the given SSRN-Elsevier format. A blind peer review process will be used to evaluate all the submitted papers. Each full registeration will cover a maximum of one paper. Extra paper, Second Paper onwards, must be registered separately. The formal instructions in the template must be followed, It is important to use the right paper format (A4 size) to have right margin, not to use page numbering (paper footer must be empty). All submissions should be written in english with a maximum paper length of 6 pages including figures. However Review Papers are allowed with maximum 8 pages.
